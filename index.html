Tech Content Specialist & DevRel

Language is my play-doh.

I’m a startup consultant with 6+ years of experience working with companies on their technical brand. I can help you find your brand’s voice and positioning, allowing you to communicate better with your audiences — both technical and non-technical.

I can also help you with content strategy and even create individual pieces of copy to support the strategy we have developed together.

Ultimately, working with me will help you become more authentic as a brand, better understood by the people you want to be heard by, and keep growing with diversity, inclusivity and a happier workforce.

I’m nonbinary, and my pronouns are they/them! I work and write in both English and German. Oh, and you can also hire me for speaking engagements! Check out my talk How To Talk About Mental Health Without Losing Your Mind!

My services

My work is situated at the intersection of tech, marketing and product. I care about your Developer Relations but also about your general company culture and the image you project externally. Instead of quick fixes, we want long-term, sustainable change that creates brand loyalty, both internally and externally.

Whether you want to call it (re)branding or DevRel — this is some of what I can do for you.

Inclusive Language Audit I take a look at your communication materials to locate language that doesn’t quite reflect the inclusivity you strive for. And then we figure out how we can improve on that.

Tone of Voice Workshop Let’s find out together what your brand sounds like and how you can communicate with your audience in a way that speaks to them. We’ll find your authentic voice and create a foundation that you can build upon in years to come.

Content Strategy Exploration Let’s develop some content ideas for your website, videos, podcasts, social media, etc. We’ll work out general themes, topics, and specific content series and categories that you can keep evolving further.

Copy Creation Whether you need entirely new copy or just want me to spruce up your existing copy, there are plenty of content types I can write for you: website copy, blog posts, explainer video scripts, ad copy, tag lines, slogans, etc.

Copy Coaching for Your Content Team Already got a fantastic copy team that just needs a bit of support? I can help your team become better writers and content producers by showing them how to approach the writing and editing process, nipping writer’s block in the bud.

DEI Community Audit Wanting to be more diverse and inclusive is a significant first step. But are you walking the walk? Let’s find out how welcome marginalised people feel — both your employees and your wider community.
